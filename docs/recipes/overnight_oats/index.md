# Overnight Oats

![image](image.jpg)

| :material-clock-outline: Time | :fork_and_knife: Servings |
|-------------------------------|---------------------------|
| 30 min                        | 6 portions                |

---

## Ingredients

###  Base

- _200g_ oats
- _1150mL_ water
- 4 scoops of protein powder
- _50g_ peanut butter

### Vanilla version

- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 12 tbsp of raspberry marmalade

### Tiramisu version

- 1 cup of espresso coffee
- 1 tsp cacao powder
- 6 crumbled cookies

### "Apfelstrudel" version

- 1 grated apple
- 6 tsp of cinnamon

---

## Instruction

1. Prepare the base. Add water, protein powder, oats, and peanut butter into a blender and blend until smooth.
2. Depending on the version, add the rest of the ingredients.
3. Pour the mixture into glass containers and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
